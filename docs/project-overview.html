PitchLens

AI-powered pitch deck analyzer that delivers investor-grade scorecards using a multi-agent architecture

⚡ Multi-Agent AI 📊 4-Dimension Scoring 🔄 Real-Time Streaming 💬 RAG-Based Chat

Use Case & Relevance

Why PitchLens exists and who it serves

Startup founders iterate on pitch decks dozens of times before meeting investors. Each iteration requires feedback — typically expensive ($500-2000/consultation) or slow (days to weeks from mentors). PitchLens automates this feedback loop with AI that thinks like a VC analyst, delivering structured, actionable scorecards in under 60 seconds.

Target Users

🚀

Startup Founders

Get instant investor-perspective feedback before pitching. Identify weak areas and iterate faster.

🎓

Accelerator Programs

Screen and evaluate cohort applications at scale. Provide structured feedback to applicants.

💼

Angel Investors

Quick initial screening of deal flow. Standardized scoring across opportunities.

🏫

Business Schools

Teaching tool for entrepreneurship courses. Students get instant AI-powered feedback on assignments.

Key Value Propositions

1

Speed

Full multi-dimensional analysis in ~60 seconds vs days/weeks from human reviewers. Parallel AI agents process all dimensions simultaneously.

2

Consistency

Standardized scoring rubric across Market, Team, Business Model, and Competition. No reviewer mood, fatigue, or bias variability.

3

Actionability

Each dimension includes specific improvement suggestions referencing actual deck content. Follow-up chat enables deeper exploration of any feedback point.

4

Cost

Democratizes access to investor-grade feedback. A single VC consultation costs $500-2000; PitchLens delivers comparable analytical depth at a fraction of the cost.

AI Scoring Dimensions

Four specialized AI agents evaluate distinct aspects of the pitch

🌍

Market Opportunity

TAM/SAM/SOM analysis, market timing, growth potential evidence, market size specificity

👥

Team Strength

Founder backgrounds, relevant domain experience, team completeness, advisory board quality

💰

Business Model

Revenue model clarity, unit economics, scalability potential, monetization strategy

⚔️

Competitive Edge

Competitive landscape awareness, differentiation strength, defensibility/moat

Scoring Output Per Dimension

FieldTypeDescription
Scoreint (1-10)Numeric evaluation on a standardized scale
Reasoningstring (50-500 words)Detailed explanation referencing deck content
Suggestionsstring[] (1-3 items)Actionable improvements specific to the deck

Security Architecture

Defense-in-depth approach across all layers

🔐

JWT Authentication

15-min access tokens + 7-day refresh tokens. Bcrypt password hashing. Stateless verification.

🛡️

Prompt Injection Guard

Pattern-based scanner blocks role-override, system-prompt extraction, and delimiter escape attacks. Fail-closed design.

⏱️

Rate Limiting

10 analyses/hour, 60 API calls/minute per user. Sliding window counters. 429 + Retry-After headers.

🔒

Data Isolation

Per-user data ownership. 403 without existence revelation. Cross-user access logging.
